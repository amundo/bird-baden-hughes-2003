Free translation:

Traditional folk songs. Besides presenting various kinds of tales (lax°nako ), lament recitatives (yarøbc° ), and heroic recitatives (syud°bøbc° ), the people, in the course of their lives, have made songs in their own minds. On the surface of the earth, only few people cannot perform words made by themselves as a song ­ they are the ones unable to perform a song. Such people are very few.
